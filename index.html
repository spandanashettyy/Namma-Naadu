📅 Sample 5-Day Karnataka Itinerary

A curated journey through the best of Karnataka

Day 1 – Bengaluru (City of Gardens)

Morning
Lalbagh Botanical Garden – Stroll through 240 acres of lush greenery and century-old trees.
Afternoon
Bangalore Palace – Tudor-style architecture inspired by Windsor Castle.
Evening
MG Road & Brigade Road – Shopping, street food, and the famous Koshy's café.

Day 2 – Mysuru (City of Palaces)

Morning
Mysore Palace – One of India's most magnificent royal palaces; arrive early to beat the crowds.
Afternoon
Chamundeshwari Temple – Hilltop temple with panoramic city views; 1,000-step climb.
Evening
Devaraja Market – Flowers, spices, and silk; a riot of colours and aromas.

Day 3 – Coorg (Scotland of India)

Morning
Abbey Falls – Misty 70-foot waterfall surrounded by coffee and pepper estates.
Afternoon
Raja's Seat – Historic sunset viewpoint used by Coorg kings.
Evening
Homestay Experience – Stay with a Kodava family; enjoy authentic pandi curry and kaad mannu kozhicurry.

Day 4 – Hampi (UNESCO World Heritage)

Morning
Virupaksha Temple – 7th-century temple still in active worship; an architectural marvel.
Afternoon
Stone Chariot & Vittala Temple – The iconic stone chariot is Karnataka's emblem on the ₹50 note.
Evening
Matanga Hill – Best sunset spot in Hampi; boulders glow golden at dusk.

Day 5 – Gokarna (Temple Town & Beaches)

Morning
Mahabaleshwar Temple – Ancient Shiva temple with the sacred Atmalinga.
Afternoon
Om Beach – Naturally shaped like the OM symbol; great for swimming and kayaking.
Evening
Half Moon & Paradise Beach – Secluded beaches accessible only by boat or trek.

🎉 Karnataka Festivals

Plan your visit around these vibrant cultural celebrations

👑
Mysuru Dasara
October
A 10-day royal extravaganza with illuminated palace, elephant procession & cultural shows. The state festival of Karnataka.
🪔
Ugadi
March / April
Kannada New Year celebrated with traditional pachadi, new clothes, temple visits, and family feasts.
🌾
Pattadakal Dance Festival
January
Classical dance performances against the backdrop of the 8th-century UNESCO Pattadakal temples.
🎭
Hampi Utsav
November
Three-day cultural festival at the Vijayanagara ruins — music, dance, puppet shows, and heritage walks.
☕
Coorg Coffee Festival
December
Celebrate the coffee harvest with estate tours, tastings, and local Kodava cuisine in the Western Ghats.
🎪
Karaga Festival
March / April
Ancient Bengaluru festival where the priest carries a flower pyramid on his head in a midnight procession.
🏖️
Gokarna Beach Festival
January
Music, yoga, cultural events, and bonfire nights on the sacred beaches of Gokarna.
🌊
Murudeshwar Sea Festival
February
Watersports, temple visits, and a celebration of Karnataka's coastal culture near the tallest Shiva statue.

🧳 Travel Tips for Karnataka

Insider advice to make your journey smooth

🗓️ Best Time to Visit

  • Oct – Feb: Best overall; pleasant weather across the state
  • Mar – May: Hot; ideal for coastal and hill stations
  • Jun – Sep: Monsoon; waterfalls and Coorg are stunning
  • Avoid peak summer in Hampi (extremely hot)

🚌 Getting Around

  • KSRTC buses connect all major cities — affordable and reliable
  • Book trains via IRCTC for inter-city travel
  • Rent a two-wheeler in Hampi, Coorg & Gokarna for flexibility
  • Bengaluru Metro (Namma Metro) for city travel
  • Auto-rickshaws for last-mile connectivity everywhere

🍛 Food to Try

  • Masala Dosa – Must-try at Vidyarthi Bhavan, Bengaluru
  • Bisi Bele Bath – Spiced lentil-rice dish unique to Karnataka
  • Pandi Curry – Coorg-style pork curry with kachampuli
  • Kadubu – Steamed rice dumplings in coconut milk
  • Filter Coffee – A Malnad ritual you cannot miss

💡 Smart Travel Tips

  • Carry a printed/offline map for remote areas like Hampi
  • Book Mysore Palace tickets online to skip queues
  • Bargain at Devaraja Market but be respectful
  • Wildlife sanctuaries require advance safari booking
  • Respect dress codes at temples (cover knees and shoulders)

🏨 Where to Stay

  • Bengaluru: Koramangala & Indiranagar for hip stays
  • Mysuru: Hotels near Palace Road for best access
  • Coorg: Homestays in Madikeri for authentic experience
  • Hampi: Guesthouses in Virupapur Gaddi (river island)
  • Gokarna: Beach huts near Om Beach

📱 Useful Apps & Links

  • KSRTC App – Bus booking across Karnataka
  • Karnataka Tourism – Official site for permits & info
  • Namma Metro App – Bengaluru metro routes & fares
  • Google Maps – Works well even in remote areas
  • Weather apps: Check Accuweather for Coorg & Malnad
